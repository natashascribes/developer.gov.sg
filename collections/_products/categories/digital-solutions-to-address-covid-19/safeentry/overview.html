Overview

As more services and activities gradually resume operations following the COVID-19 pandemic, it is crucial to put in place contact tracing systems that will enable us to combat clusters and prevent the spread of the virus.

SafeEntry is a national digital check-in system that logs identifying details of individuals (identification details and mobile numbers) visiting public venues such as malls and workplaces, as well as communal activity areas. SafeEntry supports and quicken contact tracing efforts by providing authorities with a record of individuals who visit these places.

SafeEntry has been integrated with the TraceTogether app. Users can scan the SafeEntry QR code via the TraceTogether app or tap their Bluetooth mobile devices, and check-in without having to enter their details manually.

What is SafeEntry?

SafeEntry was built to resolve the need for an effective and practical visitor management amidst COVID-19. By logging identifying details of visitors digitally, this reduces the time needed to identify potential close contacts of COVID-19 patients and clusters.

To ensure that checking in and out is quick, convenient, and accessible for all, there are several check-in methods available:

  • Scan venue QR code with TraceTogether app – SafeEntry has been integrated with the TraceTogether app. SafeEntry QR codes can be scanned through the TraceTogether app.

  • Check-in with SafeEntry Gateway – Tap the TraceTogether App or Token at a SafeEntry Gateway device. This requires a Bluetooth connection.

  • Scan QR code on TraceTogether Token – Present the QR code on your TraceTogether Token to be scanned by the staff.

Health Risk Warning (HRW)
SafeEntry and TraceTogether records are used to identity close contacts of COVID positive cases. These close contacts may then receive a HRW via SMS. For more information on HRW, click here.

Why Should SafeEntry be Adopted?

SafeEntry supports and enables both citizens and businesses, allowing the economy to open safely amidst the COVID-19 pandemic.

Businesses (Register your business here) – SafeEntry supports your business by reducing manual efforts needed to log entry and exit of each visitor so that your business can continue to operate safely.

Citizens – scanning your entry and exit helps to make contact tracing more accurate and prevent COVID-19 clusters from forming.

As more activities and services gradually resume, it is important to prevent and control the transmission of COVID-19. By streamlining and automating certain aspects of contact tracing, this allows for quicker identification of COVID-19 clusters which minimises the spread of the virus.
