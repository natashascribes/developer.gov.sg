The Networked Trade Platform (NTP) is a national trade information management platform that provides the foundation for Singapore to be the world's leading trade, supply chain and trade financing hub. At its core, it represents a concerted effort to drive an industry-wide digital transformation to build a trade and logistics IT ecosystem which connects businesses, community systems and platforms and government systems.

Its vision is to be a national trade info-ecosystem that provides the foundation for Singapore to be the world's leading trade, supply chain and trade financing hub. Replacing TradeNet for trade-related applications and TradeXchange for connecting the trade and logistics community, the NTP is designed to provide beyond the service offerings of the incumbent systems. Specifically, it aims to be a:

  • One-stop trade information management system linked to other platforms
  • Next-generation platform offering a wide range of trade-related services
  • Open innovation platform allowing development of insights & new services with cross-industry data
  • Document hub for digitisation at source that enables reuse of data to cut costs and streamline processes

Features

In line with the vision of driving Singapore to the frontier of a digitalised SmartNation with an emphasis on trade, the NTP will allow you to:

  • Engage Your Partners and Networks - Invite or add your business partners on the NTP today to establish a business connection and grow your network. In doing so, you can start collaborating by using the NTP's Data Repository tool for your business transactions
  • Explore Value-Added Services - An online marketplace showcasing innovative value-added services created by VAS Developers and VAS Providers. These services are built on the NTP's open development platform to meet varied business needs, including business services that cut across adjacent sectors such as the provision of trade financing.
  • Manage Your Documents on Data Repository - An online cloud-based repository offering digitisation, uploading and storage services for documents. These documents can be seamlessly, swiftly and securely shared to business partners or government organisations when needed.
  • Establish Digital Trade Connectivity - Streamline your document sharing by connecting directly to the NTP with a SFTP connection, or choose API integration for a more dynamic and to-date connectivity.
  • Monitor Activities with Your Dashboard - An integrated overview of notifications, pending and outstanding tasks for you to keep track of all your activities on the NTP. Customise your dashboard to services most important to you so that you can immediately access your commonly used services on the NTP at one go.
  • Transact using Trade-related Government Services - Your gateway to handling trade declarations and related matters with Singapore Customs and Competent Authorities. Watch this space for more services that will be rolled out progressively on the NTP.
  • Engage using Social Features - Engage members of the Trade & Logistics industry through Blogs, Communities and Forums, and discover new business development possibilities via Opportunities. Watch this space for future developments.
  • Innovate with the Developer Portal - Leverage innovative infrastructure and toolkits to help design and build applications for the NTP. Developers can look forward to tools such as Data APIs, Microservices, and a developer sandbox. Watch this space for future developments.

Get Started

Signing in to Networked Trade Platform (NTP) with your registered CorpPass account enables you to experience the full suite of services and functionalities enabled on NTP. You will have access to business services that enable and ease business processes and collaboration, such as:

  • Managing company users and keeping your profile up-to date
  • Expanding your business network by establishing business connections with your business partners on NTP
  • Uplifting your digital capabilities by subscribing to value-added services on NTP
  • Managing and sharing your documents digitally with your business partners for business and trade-related transactions
