Overview

OneMap is the authoritative national map of Singapore with the most detailed and updated information developed by the Singapore Land Authority. There are also many useful day-to-day information and services contributed by government agencies.

OneMap provides APIs for developers to build on top of its services such as location search, planning area data and routing.
