Service Layer

The Services Layer comprises rapidly evolving and widely applicable, reusable service components including specialised Tech Stacks such as Analytics, Design, Digital Identity, IoT, Data Science and Data Transfer. The vision is to enable easy discovery of services in a common API Exchange (APEX) marketplace that is slated for pilot in Q2 FY22. Standardising communication within Agency systems and consumers (including citizen, businesses, and government) promotes collaborative experiences and reduces complexity.

Following are a set of Service Layer products available. We are currently streamlining access and designing a governance model:

Analytics

Personalise

Email and web personalisation tools that help agencies better analyse user behaviours and provide tailored experiences to individuals.

WOG Application Analytics (WOGAA)

Analytics & performance platform for public officers to monitor the health of their government websites and optimise the performance of their digital services with data.

Design

Singapore Government Design System

Create fast, accessible, and mobile-friendly digital services with design templates.

Digital Identity

Singpass Login

Password-less login method for users to access public and private sector digital services using the Singpass app.

Corppass

Corporate digital identity for businesses and other entities to conduct online transactions with Government agencies.

Myinfo

“Tell Us Once” service that pre-fills personal data retrieved from government sources for online transactions.

Verify

Enables users to securely perform in-person identity verification and data sharing, through scanning of QR codes with their Singpass app.

Identiface

A secure authentication method where users verify their identities through facial scanning.

Sign

Enables users to digitally sign documents using their Singpass app.

Internet of Things

DECADA Cloud

Secure IoT platform for device management and sensor data analysis.

DECADA Edge Gateway

Onboards new and existing sensor nodes to DECADA easily and securely.

DECADA Embedded

A family of microcontroller-class devices that can communicate directly to DECADA Cloud.

Data Science

Video Analytics

A central platform for Agencies to develop in-house video analytics solutions.

Data Transfer

Cloud File Transfer

API-driven managed file transfer service that enables agencies to conduct secure and compliant cross-zone file transfer.
