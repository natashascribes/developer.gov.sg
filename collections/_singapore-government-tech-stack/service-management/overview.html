The following are service management tools that agencies can use to discover, subscribe to and manage Singapore Government Tech Stack (SGTS) services.

Engineering Suite

Engineering Suite is a service management tool that will help agencies to subscribe to and manage SGTS services from a single console.

TechPass

TechPass is an Identity & Access Management (IAM) and Single Sign On (SSO) Solution for services within the Singapore Government Technology Stack (SGTS).
