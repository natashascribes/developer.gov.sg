The Engineering Suite Overview

Defining the Engineering Suite

Singapore Government Tech Stack (SGTS) offers a set of reusable components to streamline application development for agile system delivery. If there was a one-stop shop for you to discover, sign up and manage all the SGTS services, won't it be convenient? Introducing the Engineering Suite - the service management tool that will help agencies to subscribe and manage SGTS services from a single console.

A Singapore Government Tech Stack (SGTS) Product

The Engineering Suite belongs to a suite of Service Management components within the Base Layer of the SGTS.

Key features*

Engineering Suite aims to unify all SGTS services to make it easy for agencies to adopt the stack. We will be rolling out tools within Engineering Suite for:

Single Sign-On, Identity & Access Management solution

Enable users to access services and seamlessly transition between different SGTS services.

TechPass Beta Coming Soon

Monitoring & Metrics

Consolidates the usage metrics of SGTS services. The data is represented based on the profile of different stakeholders.

Seamless Onboarding

Simplifies contract management and speeds up the onboarding process by removing repetitive steps and reusing the information we already have.

Support Service

A feedback & help centre that oversees and facilitates the “front-of-house” support for SGTS services.
